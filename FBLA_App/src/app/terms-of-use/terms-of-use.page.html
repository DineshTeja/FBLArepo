1. THE APPLICATION

FBLA District VII (hereinafter: Application) is a piece of software created to to allow for chapter sponsors to manage their local chapters by allowing for them to keep track of member attendance and major events. This would allow for more efficient management of local chapters and help keep members more informed. - and customized for Apple mobile devices. It is used to to allow for members and chapter sponsors to collectively keep track of major events in their districts and meeting attendance. This allows for information about the national FBLA organization, more information about current events, as well as important information about local chapters..
